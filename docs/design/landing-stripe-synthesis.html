Response letter · preview

14 Maple St, Mattapan

Item 1 · Owner-occupancy condition

The corrections letter conditions the ADU permit on owner-occupancy of the primary dwelling. State law preempts this condition. Per state statute, no municipality may require the property owner to occupy either the primary dwelling or the ADU. MGL Ch 40A §3 ↗

Item 2 · Parking exemption

The proposed ADU sits within 0.5 miles walking distance of Forest Hills Station. State law requires zero parking for ADUs in this proximity zone. St. 2024, c. 150 §8 ↗

Item 3 · Article 80 review

The proposed ADU is 712 sq ft — far below the 20,000 sq ft Small Project Review threshold. Article 80 review does not apply. Boston Zoning Art. 80 ↗
