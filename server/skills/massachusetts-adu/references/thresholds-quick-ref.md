# MA ADU Thresholds — Quick Reference

Numbers-only card. The agent should be able to answer common threshold questions from this file without loading anything else.

## Size Limits (State Floor)

| Metric | Limit | Source |
|--------|-------|--------|
| Max ADU size (absolute) | **900 sq ft** | MGL Ch 40A §1A as amended by St. 2024, c. 150, §7 |
| Max ADU size (relative) | **50% of primary dwelling's gross floor area** | Same |
| Effective cap | **Lesser of the two** | Same |

Examples:
- Primary 1,200 sq ft → ADU cap 600 sq ft (50% is less than 900)
- Primary 2,000 sq ft → ADU cap 900 sq ft (50% is 1,000, exceeds absolute cap)
- Primary 3,500 sq ft → ADU cap 900 sq ft (capped at absolute)

**Note:** Cities may impose SMALLER caps via local bylaw. They cannot impose LARGER caps on a single Protected Use ADU.

## Parking (State Ceiling)

| Scenario | Max Parking Required | Source |
|----------|---------------------|--------|
| Standard | **1 additional space** | MGL Ch 40A §3 as amended by St. 2024, c. 150, §8 |
| Within 0.5 mi of transit (commuter rail, subway, ferry, bus station) | **0 additional spaces** | Same |

Cities cannot require MORE than this. They may require less.

Open interpretive question: does "bus station" include standard bus stops? Conservative reading = no (only designated terminals). Aggressive reading = yes (any stop with regular service). When in doubt, measure to the nearest designated transit facility and apply the 0.5 mi rule.

## Occupancy

| Rule | Status | Source |
|------|--------|--------|
| Owner occupancy of primary or ADU | **Cannot be required** | MGL Ch 40A §3 as amended |
| Family relationship (e.g., "in-law apartment" only) | **Cannot be required** | Implicit — the statute says "accessory dwelling unit" period |
| Short-term rental | **May be restricted or prohibited by city** | Explicit in statute |

## Zoning Classification

| District Type | ADU By-Right? |
|--------------|---------------|
| Single-family residential | **YES, by-right** (state preemption) |
| Two-family / multi-family | Not state-preempted; depends on local zoning |
| Mixed-use / commercial | Not state-preempted; depends on local zoning |
| Agricultural | Not state-preempted; depends on local zoning |
| Industrial | Generally not allowed |

## Number of ADUs Per Lot

| Scenario | Status |
|----------|--------|
| 1st ADU in single-family zone | **By-right** |
| 2nd+ ADU | **Requires special permit** under general zoning rules |
